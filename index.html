Art Compiler is a studio doing work in the space between art and code. Our mission is to create, from language, visual works of practical and aesthetic value. Our practice is built on technical expertise acquired through decades of language design and compiler development. At the core of our work process is the construction of specialized compilers that translate language into visual representation. This means that each piece is one in a potentially infinite series of pieces produced from other texts of the same language. We find this simply facinating.
