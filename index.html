Art Compiler is a studio doing work in the space between art and code. Our mission is to create, from language, visual works of practical and aesthetic value. Our practice is built on technical expertise acquired through decades of language design and compiler development. At the core of our work are specialized compilers that translate language into meaningful objects and behaviors. Each piece is one in a potentially infinite series of related pieces generated from texts of the same language. So for us, the art is the compiler.

Our current focus is on code cartography, the mapping of program behavior onto a syntactic layout of code. We are particularly interested in work involving large JavaScript code bases.
