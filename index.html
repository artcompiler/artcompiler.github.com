Art Compiler is a studio doing work in the space between art and code. Our mission is to create, from language, visual works of practical and aesthetic value. Our practice is built on technical expertise acquired through decades of language design and compiler development.

Compilers are our art. At the core of our work are specialized compilers that translate language into meaningful objects and behaviors. Each piece is one in a potentially infinite series of related pieces generated from texts of the same language.

Code cartography is our focus. This is the mapping of program behavior onto a syntactic layout of code. We are particularly interested in work involving large dynamic language code bases, as they generally contain a wealth of hidden meaning.
