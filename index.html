Art Compiler does work in the space between art and code. Our purpose is to use language to create content of practical and aesthetic value. Our practice is built on technical expertise acquired through decades of language design and compiler development, and is driven by the desire to use computing as a platform for expressing meaning in the world.

Language is our medium. At the core of our work are specialized compilers that translate language into expressive objects. Each language defines a domain specific mapping between code and form. It is precisely this mapping that gives meaning to code and which we care about most.

Art is what you do. You are the domain experts and content providers. You are the writers, designers, educators, programmers, architects and artists who produce beautiful and useful objects for others.

Compilers for content synthesis and assessment is what we do. We automate creative processes to improve quality, increase capacity and reduce costs.

You use creative processes. We automate creative processes. Would be fun to talk.
