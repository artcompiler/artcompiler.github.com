Math Education Specialist
Art Compiler is looking for a part-time Math Education Specialist to help make the world's greatest math assessment engine for the world's leading publishers of on-line education content.

In this job you will:
•  Develop a map of skills required to perform K12 math
•  Write math assessments to test and demonstrate new features
•  Identify gaps between the skills map and existing functionality
•  Develop "user stories" that illustrate the need for new features

The person we hire will:
•  Know K-12 mathematics like the back of her/his hand
•  Have experience teaching/tutoring high school math
•  Be pursuing or have completed a degree in math education
•  Want to shape the future of computing in education

This is a part-time (~10 hours per week) contract position located on University Hill in Boulder. The pay will be competitive and commensurate with the candidate's experience.

If you feel like you are a match with this position, please write a brief note to Jeff at artcompiler dot com telling him why you think you are the one.
